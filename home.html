About Us

A hamster is the answer!

We've noticed that bigger pets get all the attention and so we'd like to focus on the little ones.

For 6 years now, we've been a growing Canadian business that's been passionate about hamsters.
Every day we learn more about them and we'd like to share our knowledge with those who are interested.
We try our best to raise and rescue as many furry friends as we can, helping them find families to go back to.

Just because these rascals are small,
doesn't mean that they don't have big hearts to love!

Join us on our journey at Hampter Rescue!

Animal Cruelty

Think that someone you know is abusing or neglecting their pet? Want to report the issue?
Here at Hampter Rescue we believe that every life, big or small, deserves a loving home.

To report any sort of animal cruelty, please call: 1-833-9-ANIMAL.

Here are a list of websites that can help inform you on the cause:
